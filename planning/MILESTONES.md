# Milestones

## M1: Foundation

**Target window:** 2 to 4 weeks

**Goal:** Know the characters, month, physical world, and governing rules well enough to prevent avoidable continuity errors.

Exit criteria:

- Character bible complete for every important character
- Day-by-day outbreak calendar covers the full novel
- Story rules reviewed and accepted
- Bennett house layout and spatial continuity documented
- Town and hospital guides sufficiently researched
- Infection behavior matrix internally consistent
- Major open canon decisions resolved or deliberately marked unknowable

## M2: Story Design

**Goal:** Produce a complete, clue-safe plan for a 30-to-40-chapter novel.

Exit criteria:

- Character arcs mapped
- Clue ledger covers every major reveal
- Scene inventory is complete
- Every chapter has purpose, conflict, reveal, hidden clue, and character movement
- Found-media inserts are planned and remain near 10 to 15 percent
- Word-count projection falls between 85,000 and 100,000 words
- Continuity audit passes before prose drafting begins

## M3: First Draft

**Goal:** Complete a coherent beginning, middle, and ending at 85,000 to 100,000 words.

Exit criteria:

- Prologue and all chapters drafted
- Weekly drafting log maintained, targeting 2,000 to 3,000 words
- No known placeholder scenes or unresolved structural gaps
- Canon, timeline, and clue ledger updated as drafting discoveries are accepted

## M4: Revision

**Goal:** Turn the completed draft into a submission- or production-ready manuscript.

Exit criteria:

- Cooling period completed
- Developmental rewrite completed
- Character, pacing, continuity, mystery, and prose passes completed
- Beta feedback collected and triaged
- Professional developmental edit, copy edit, and proofreading considered or completed as appropriate

## M5: Publication

**Goal:** Prepare the chosen publication path without prematurely locking the decision.

Exit criteria:

- Traditional, self-publishing, or sequential strategy selected
- Query package or self-publishing production package complete
- Cover, interior formatting, metadata, and marketing plan complete for the selected path
